What is it?

MyResourcer is a tool that helps you easily find and request the resources you need for your classroom or lab. Whether you need a laptop for a presentation or equipment for a project, you can track your requests and see what’s available in real-time. Your digital toolkit is just a click away; Browse equipment, manage your profile, or check your borrowing history. MyResourcer handles the heavy lifting by keeping your records organized and secure, giving you peace of mind so you can focus on what really matters.
